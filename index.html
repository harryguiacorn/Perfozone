Company Shares Selection Strategy - Performance Report (CSSS)


The sophisticated Company Shares Selection Strategy Performance Report (CSSS) operates exclusively in our Perfozone blockchain platform. The CSSS score’s indicator are the results of analytical performance reports that analyses the listed stocks strictly based on results of some financial ratios of the listed companies including: Quick Ratio, Debt to Equity Ratio, Operating Profit Margin, Return on Assets, Return on Equity, P/E Ratio, P/BV Ratio, and EPS Growth.

Our CSSS solution-based platform can offer the best investment opportunities based upon stocks aggressive growth profile, high level equity ratio, countercyclical characteristics that exhibiting relatively high barriers entry such as water and power; healthcare; pharmaceutical; media, telecom; oil and gas; financial services.

With strong management and associate technical teams behind CSSS system solution, we expect to be able to accumulate large pool of investors who wish to explore our zero management fees and secure platform.
